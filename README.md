## ‚ú® Features

### üéØ Tax-Efficient Asset Location
Automatically optimize which assets go in which accounts (taxable, traditional IRA, Roth IRA) to minimize your tax burden. Balanced understands:
- Bonds prefer tax-deferred accounts (ordinary income)
- Stocks prefer taxable accounts (qualified dividends, long-term capital gains)
- High-growth assets prefer Roth accounts (tax-free growth)

### üìä Smart Rebalancing Strategies
Choose from three rebalancing philosophies:
- **Strict/Calendar**: Rebalance on any deviation (best for tax-advantaged accounts)
- **Drift Bands (5/25 Rule)**: Only rebalance when drift exceeds thresholds (reduces unnecessary trading)
- **Inflow Only**: Never sell, only use new contributions (perfect for taxable accounts)

### üõ°Ô∏è Privacy-Focused

### üí∞ Emergency Fund Management

## üéì Learn More

### Tax-Efficient Asset Location
Balanced implements research-backed strategies for placing assets in the right account types:
- **Taxable accounts**: Tax-efficient equity index funds (qualified dividends, long-term capital gains)
- **Traditional IRA/401(k)**: Bonds and REITs (ordinary income shielded from taxes)
- **Roth IRA**: High-growth stocks (tax-free growth forever)

### Drift Band Rebalancing (5/25 Rule)
Instead of rebalancing on every deviation, Balanced uses the 5/25 rule:
- **5% absolute drift** for major assets (‚â•20% of portfolio)
- **25% relative drift** for minor assets (<20% of portfolio)

This reduces unnecessary trading while keeping your portfolio on track.

### Roth Growth Strategy
Maximize tax-free growth by prioritizing high-growth equities in Roth accounts and bonds in traditional accounts.
